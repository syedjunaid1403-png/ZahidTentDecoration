**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
# Zahid Tent Decorators Design System
**Visual Language:**
- Premium, high-converting, elegant.
- Clean typography and structured layouts.
- Dynamic micro-animations for user interaction.
**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep Maroon (`#6A1B1A`)
- Accent: Gold (`#D4AF37`)
- Background: Off-white/Beige (`#F9F6F1`)
- Text: Dark Charcoal (`#222222`)
**Typography:**
- Headings: Playfair Display
- Body: Poppins

**Page Structure:**
1. **Hero** — Full-screen with background image, headline, CTAs, trust signals, floating gold particles
2. **About** — Business intro with image, 10+ years badge, animated counters (500+ events, 100% satisfaction, 24/7)
3. **Services** — 6 cards: Wedding Tents, Birthday/Party, Function Setup, Mattress/Carpet, Stage/Lighting, Custom
4. **Why Choose Us** — 5 USP items with icons + showcase image
5. **Gallery** — 6 images with category filters (All/Weddings/Parties/Traditional), hover zoom, lightbox
6. **Testimonials** — 3 customer reviews with star ratings
7. **Contact** — Phone, WhatsApp, address, hours + embedded Google Map pointing to Zahid Tent House
8. **Footer** — Quick links, services, contact info
9. **Sticky CTA** — Floating call + WhatsApp buttons (bottom-right)
